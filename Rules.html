Welcome to Phoenix Tag Scavenger Hunt

Phoenix Tag Scavenger Hunt features an interactive cybersecurity scavenger hunt that uses tools including: NFC tags, Google Maps, and challenging cybersecurity-based questions to help players learn all about secure software concepts and topics.

How does our Game Work?

  1. First create your account or log in. Your account will help track your score on the leaderboard.
  2. Start the game. The game opens Google Maps API and your first question.
  3. Answer 5 randomized questions. Questions are pulled at random from a list of topics.
  4. Earn points. Correct answers increase your score. Incorrect answers decrease your score.
  5. Check in with the zone. Correct answers shrink the zone.
  6. Find the final NFC tag. After 5 questions, scan the final NFC tag.
  7. Claim your prize. Press Claim to finish and view your leaderboard placement.

Rules

  • Your not aloud to skip directly to the final NFC tag.
  • You must answer all questions before claiming your prize.
  • You may only use your account to play.
  • Stay within safe areas and check your surroundings when playing.
  • You are to not remove, damage, or move any NFC tags.

Learning Goal

This game reinforces secure software engineering topics such as input validation, least privilege, secure development practices, common software vulnerabilities, etc.
